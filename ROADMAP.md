## Pending

### Batch 5 — Social Layer
- 1v1 Challenges (lift-off between two users on the same exercise)
- Group Challenges (group volume goal over a set period)
- Muscle Balance Map (radar chart showing relative muscle development)
- Hydration Tracker (daily water intake log + goal)

### Batch 9 — Unit Toggle
- kg / lb toggle applied consistently across sessions, diet, progress, dashboard
- km / mile toggle for cardio distance

---

## Upcoming

### Batch 10 — Analytics & Charts
- PR history chart per exercise (line graph showing progression over time)
- Volume over time chart per muscle group
- Body weight trend line (once Batch 11 lands)
- Strength standards comparison (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced / Elite for bodyweight)

### Batch 11 — Body Tracking
- Daily weigh-in with trend graph
- Body measurements log (chest, waist, arms, quads) over time
- Progress photos with side-by-side date comparison

### Batch 12 — Smarter Training
- Auto-generated warmup sets based on working weight (50%×10, 70%×5, 85%×3)
- Progressive overload suggestions ("last week: 185×8 — try 190 today")
- Superset / circuit mode (group exercises, alternate between them)
- RPE or RIR logging per set

### Batch 13 — Nutrition Upgrades
- Barcode scanner for packaged food (Open Food Facts barcode endpoint + camera)
- Copy yesterday's meals with one tap
- Full-day meal plan templates / recurring meals

### Batch 14 — Recovery & Wellness
- Sleep log (hours + quality rating per night)
- Recovery readiness score (sleep + days since last session + weekly volume)

### Batch 15 — Convenience & Reliability
- Export workout history to CSV
- Today's Plan widget — shows today's split muscles + last session's numbers before starting
- Offline-first session logging with background sync when connection restores

### Batch 16 — AI Coaching (Claude API)
- Workout recommendations based on muscle frequency, recovery score, and split
- Stall detection ("your bench has been flat for 3 weeks — here's what to change")
- Progressive overload coaching with periodization awareness
